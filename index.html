Digital Exclusion Risk in the EU

Country-level scrollytelling combining web accessibility barriers (WebAIM), digital capacity (DESI), and sight-loss burden by gender (Vision Atlas).

Hover a country to view details and cross-highlight across visualizations.

1) Map: Where is the selected metric concentrated?

This map shows the selected metric across the EU-27. Use it to spot spatial patterns quickly.

2) Ranking: Who leads and who lags?

Countries sorted by the selected metric. This helps compare extremes and identify outliers.

3) Trade-offs: Web barriers vs digital context

Scatterplot of WASS (higher = more barriers) vs Digital context (higher = more capacity). Bubble size reflects the female sight-loss burden.

4) Gender dimension: Female vs male sight-loss burden

Ratio of female to male sight-loss burden by country. Values above 1 indicate higher female burden.

Methodology & limitations

  • WASS aggregates WebAIM error density and contrast issues (normalized).
  • Digital context uses DESI ICT specialists as a proxy (normalized).
  • IDFDV rewards accessibility + capacity, adjusted by female burden.
  • DERVIW composes risk factors using a geometric mean and is re-normalized to 0–1.
  • All results are relative within the EU-27 (comparative, not absolute).
